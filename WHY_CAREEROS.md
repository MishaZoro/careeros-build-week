# Why CareerOS Exists

Professional value is created every day but remembered inconsistently. It lives in projects, work orders, customer outcomes, certifications, reviews, training, and hard-won lessons—then gets reconstructed only when a person is already under pressure.

That timing is backwards. A resume captures selected history. CareerOS preserves Career DNA: trusted evidence, context, outcomes, limitations, and growth. It helps the Career Owner understand what their experience supports now, adjust direction honestly, and build the next proof before the next urgent career moment.

CareerOS is an adaptive companion because careers are not one application, one employer, or one degree. It remains useful through vocational training, apprenticeship, advancement, switching, leadership, consulting, retirement, and legacy.

CareerOS learns the professional, not the profession.
