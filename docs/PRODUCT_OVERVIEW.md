# CareerOS Product Overview

## In 15 seconds

CareerOS is a lifelong AI career companion and evidence-grounded adviser. It turns professional experience into governed Career DNA, explains what that evidence supports today, and identifies what to build next.

## Problem and solution

Professional evidence is created continuously but reconstructed only when someone is under pressure. A resume summarizes selected history; CareerOS preserves the evidence, context, limits, and growth behind it. The current MVP proves deterministic readiness analysis, Career Horizon, session-only achievement capture, and traceable next actions.

## Companion and adviser

The companion provides continuity through discovery, training, work, advancement, adjustment, switching, consulting, retirement, and legacy. The adviser interprets evidence without replacing coaches, mentors, managers, educators, or human judgment.

## Inclusion and business model

CareerOS is degree-neutral. Vocational, apprenticeship, technical, military, certification, and on-the-job evidence can support advancement when supported by responsibility and outcomes. The direct user is the Career Owner. Individuals are the trust anchor; universities, vocational schools, technical colleges, apprenticeship programs, employers, workforce organizations, associations, transition providers, coaches, and retirement programs are the B2B2C distribution model.

## Boundaries

The Build Week MVP is local and deterministic. It does not yet provide production AI, persistence, resume generation, interview generation, job ingestion, billing, authentication, or integrations.
