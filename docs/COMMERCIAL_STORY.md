# CareerOS Commercial Story

## Product and customer

CareerOS is a lifelong career companion with an evidence-grounded advisory layer. The initial customer is **the Career Owner**: a person at any stage facing a meaningful career decision who wants portable ownership of their evidence, readiness, direction, and future options.

The individual is the trust anchor. Institutions are the distribution and recurring-revenue engine. Longitudinal Career DNA is the moat.

## Pain and solution

Professional evidence is created continuously but remembered inconsistently, scattered across systems, and reconstructed only under pressure. CareerOS captures governed evidence while work happens, explains what it credibly supports, preserves limitations, and identifies the next proof to build.

## Buyers and model

- **B2C:** a Career Owner uses or purchases CareerOS directly and owns their Career DNA.
- **B2B2C:** universities, vocational and technical schools, community colleges, apprenticeship and union programs, workforce organizations, employers, associations, transition providers, coaches, and retirement programs sponsor access without taking ownership.
- **Pricing hypothesis:** free evidence entry; individual monthly/annual subscription or career-event package; institution-sponsored cohort access. Validate before pricing commitment.

## Inclusion, adjustment, and future outputs

CareerOS is degree-neutral. Vocational, technical, military, apprenticeship, certification, and on-the-job evidence can support advancement when responsibility, outcomes, and required credentials are present. It supports role adjustment, industry adjustment, functional switch, leadership, return-to-work, consulting, and retirement transitions without treating transferable skills as substitutes for licenses or regulated qualifications.

Resume tailoring and interview preparation are **future evidence-grounded outputs**, not current Build Week features. They would select defensible Career DNA, preserve Direct/Adjacent boundaries, and show lineage rather than invent stories.

## Distinction, moat, and why now

Other tools present a career moment. CareerOS helps users build, adjust, understand, and carry the career itself. Its moat is trusted longitudinal Career DNA, evidence lineage, limitation-aware reasoning, user portability, cross-lifecycle reuse, and institutional distribution. Less-linear careers, fragmented evidence, alternative pathways, longer working lives, and practical AI interpretation make this timely; governance makes it trustworthy.

## First validation experiments

Interview 10–15 Career Owners across vocational, early-career, career-change, management, consulting, and retirement stages; test evidence habits, urgency, trust, and willingness to pay. Interview institutional buyers on privacy, outcomes, budget owner, cohort access, and renewal. Test user-owned versus institution-owned framing and acquisition interest in future resume/interview outputs.
